A little story about a young man.

I'm a young man living in Madina.

I was born in Bawjiase. Due to one or two reasons my parents divorced after i was conceived and I'm the eldest child. So i was living in a room with my late Grandma who passed away last two years July 31st, 2022.
My dad was in the same house with us tho but in a different
room with his wife which he later married after the divorce
of him and my mum.

I didn't really get it easy as a kid growing up in terms of
financial assistance. I could see my Dad really trying but
things wasn't easy, sometimes I run into my room and cry for hours not because I was hungry or beaten but because I see my Dad crying.

My parents are the strongest, loving and caring people I
know in this world.
Seeing my Dad [The Strongest Man I Know] shedding tears was not easy for me to handle. I never asked him why but as a child and to my state of living I knew the reason. Plus I heard a conversation my Grandpa had with him.

Due to that I let my father talked to her elder sister who's
living in Agona Swedru, that I want to go and live with her
so that even if they'll turn me to house help without paying
me, my financial assistance would be easy.
I didn't opened up like that to my dad tho, I just told him I want to go live with my aunt so he should tell her for them to see when she'll come and take me.

My father discussed that with her and she agreed so I
migrated to Swedru and lived with her in 2010.
I spent three years there and came back to Bawjiase. I was able to tolerate everything she did to me cos I knew my Dad
don't have. Tho my aunt is really a good person but she has
mood swings and as a kid I didn't know that at that time.

I wasn't sent off from my aunt's house but I run from there,
went to beg someone money to use it as a cab fare. It was
2013. The moment I alighted in the cab when I got
to Bawjiase, I saw my Dad in a trotro car driving. I went to
him and told him everything. he gave me money to buy
food and asked me to go home, I did so.

My father already knew what was going on so he didn't
allow me to finish talking. I remember he once came to
Swedru and quarreled with my aunt that I'm also her child.
I couldn't wait to complete even J.H.S due to what was
going on. My aunt has a really wonderful husband. He was
the reason why I was able to stay there for three years.

I was a shop keeper tho, my aunt had a shop. So in BJ
I came to continue my education and I completed J.H.S
in 2014.
I had two friends in the school and we applied for
schorlaship, I waited for a year and didn't see
anything so again I planned to go live my Dad's twin
sister who's living in Madina.
For that one I didn't tell my Dad anything, I just wanted
to move from Bawjiase, go hustle somewhere to earn money
and further my education, that was the plan.

My Dad only got to know about it after I was gone. In Madina I told my aunt about my plans so she took me her
friend who was selling slippers that if he could employ me
and pay me monthly. but her friend suggested it would be
better if i learn handwork.
They discussed that for a while and my aunt asked me if I
like the idea, I was cool with that so I agreed. My aunt's
friend called a man on phone who later became my Boss
and asked the price and everything they take if a person
wants to learn their work.

Few days after, I started learning the handwork which was
Tiling & Plumbing. I came to like it as I started knowing
and understanding how it is.
Middle of 2016 I got a call from one of my friends I applied the schorlaship with, that it has come. I couldn't
go back again because of the handwork I was learning.
